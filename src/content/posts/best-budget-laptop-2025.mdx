## Why you need a good machine (and not a RM10k one)

<div className="justify">
As a developer, your laptop is your primary tool. But here is the truth: **you don't need a RM10,000 MacBook to write good code.**

In 2025, the gap between a RM3,500 laptop and a premium machine has narrowed significantly. Most of that "luxury" price goes toward 8K video editing power or brand tax. For coding, you just need a machine that removes **friction** — no laggy editors, no slow Docker starts, and no overheating during a long compile. Keep in mind, prices may vary over time.

## Finding your "sweet spot" in Malaysia

When shopping in the local market, the **RM2,800 to RM4,000** range is the Goldilocks Zone:

- **RM2,800 – RM3,200** — Best for web dev (HTML/CSS, React, PHP/Laravel)
- **RM3,300 – RM4,000** — Best for mobile dev (Ionic, Flutter) or backend (Java/Spring) — you get better H-series processors

> **Developer pro-tip: why 16GB is the "new 8GB"**
>
> In 2025, don't even look at 8GB laptops. Between Chrome tabs, Slack, VS Code, and a local database, 8GB will force your machine into swap memory and slow you down. Always prioritize RAM over a fancy screen.
